# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** January 1, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Merchandice ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

- **Information you provide:** If you contact us, you may provide your name, email address, and message content.
- **Automatic information:** We may collect basic usage data such as browser type, pages visited, and timestamps.

## How We Use Information

- To respond to inquiries.
- To operate and improve the website.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to understand traffic and improve the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help operate the site, or if required by law.

## Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

## Your Choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at [contactus@merchandice.in](mailto:contactus@merchandice.in).
